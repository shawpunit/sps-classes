About SPS Classes

At SPS Classes, we believe that clarity in concepts is the foundation of academic excellence. Led by Sir Punit Shaw, our institute is dedicated to providing high-quality, result-oriented education through both offline and online modes. We specialize in simplifying the complexities of Science and Mathematics, helping students build confidence and achieve their highest potential.

Our Academic Programs

  • Middle School (Classes 6 – 8): Comprehensive coaching for Science and Mathematics.
  • Secondary School (Classes 9 – 10): In-depth preparation for Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Master board-exam patterns.
  • Senior Secondary (Classes 11 – 12): Specialized coaching exclusively for Physics.

Why Choose SPS Classes?

  • Hybrid Learning: Offline classroom or interactive online sessions.
  • Expert Guidance: Benefit from Sir Punit Shaw’s dedicated teaching methodology.
  • Concept-Driven Approach: Focusing on "why" and "how".
